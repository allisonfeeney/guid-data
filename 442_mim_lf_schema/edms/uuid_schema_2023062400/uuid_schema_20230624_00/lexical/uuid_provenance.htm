uuid_provenance

A guid_provenance is the specification of a sequence of guid_relationships that provides historical record of those relationshps. The sequence is a simple list form.

EXPRESS specification:

ENTITY uuid_provenance;
content  :  LIST [1:?]OF UNIQUE uuid_relationship;
identifier  :  uuid;
UNIQUE
UR1  :  identifier;
END_ENTITY;
