uuid_relationship_role

A guid_relationship_role enumerates the permitted roles associated with a guid_relationship.

EXPRESS specification:

TYPE uuid_relationship_role = ENUMERATION OF
( SUPERSEDES,
MERGE,
SPLIT,
DERIVE_FROM,
SAME_AS,
SIMILAR_TO);
END_TYPE;
